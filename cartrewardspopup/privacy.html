Privacy Policy

App: Cart Rewards Popup

This Privacy Policy explains how Inovation (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Cart Rewards Popup application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

Merchant Data

When you install the App, we may access and store:

  • Shop name and shop domain
  • Email address associated with the Shopify account
  • App configuration data, such as reward rules and settings

Customer Data

The App does not collect or store personal customer data such as names, email addresses, or payment information. Cart data is processed only to evaluate reward eligibility in real time.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the App
  • Validate reward eligibility
  • Maintain app functionality and security
  • Provide support and communicate with merchants

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. Information may be shared only:

  • As required to operate the App within the Shopify platform
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers used to host or operate the App, under confidentiality obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Data is stored only for as long as necessary to operate the App.

5. Data Retention

Merchant data is retained while the App is installed. After the App is uninstalled, data is deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period, unless retention is required by law.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use third-party tracking cookies. Any analytics used are limited to app functionality and performance and do not identify individual customers.

7. Your Rights

Merchants may request access to or deletion of their data by contacting support@inovation.app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@inovation.app.
